What Is A Bestiary?

A bestiary is a historical collection of real or myhtical animals. It usually has illistrations or pictures of what the beasts either looked like or may have looked like.

What is Middle-Earth & Nümenor?

Middle-Earth & Nümenor related to the lands dreamt up and created by the author John Ronald Reuel Tolkien, or J.R.R. Tolkien, who wrote the novels:

  • The Hobbit
  • Lord of the Rings: Fellowship of the Ring
  • Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers
  • Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King
  • The Silmarillion
  • Unfinished Tales of Nümenor & Middle-Earth

The lands of Middle-Earth in simple terms are the lands that man, or humams, inhabited along with Elves, Dwarves, Hobbits, and other creatures such as dragons during the third-age of Tolkien's world. While Nümenor was a large star shaped island the used to exist to the west of Middle-Earth during the second-age.
